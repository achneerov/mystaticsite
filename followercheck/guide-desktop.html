How to Use My Website on Desktop

This is a simple guide to using my website; it will take less than five minutes to complete.

  1. 1. First, go to instagram.com, and go to your home page.

  2. 2. Click the three dashes icon.

  3. 3. Click the settings icon.

  4. 4. Click the accounts center.

  5. 5. Click your information and permissions.

  6. 6. Click download your information.

  7. 7. Click request a download.

  8. 8. Select Instagram only, and click next.

  9. 9. Select types of information.

  10. 10. Select followers and following.

  11. 11. Select next.

  12. 12. Select date range.

  13. 13. Select all time.

  14. 14. Select save.

  15. 15. Click format.

  16. 16. Click JSON.

  17. 17. Click save.

  18. 18. Click submit request.

  19. 19. IMPORTANT, it may take a few minutes for them to prepare your download, you will know it is ready when they send you an email. Once they send the email, refresh the page.

  20. 20. Once you receive the email and reload the page, it should look like this.

  21. 21. Then click download.

  22. 22. Now go back to the Home page and select choose file.

  23. 23. Then select the connections folder.

  24. 24. Then select followers and following.

  25. 25. Then select followers_1.json or something similar.

  26. 26. Then click upload.

  27. 27. Repeat steps 22-26 with the other upload field and upload the following.json file, then click compare.

  28. 28. Done! You will see a list of everyone who is not following you back below.
